Tolmin - Solwenien

One of the borders of the Triglav National Park, embraced by the rivers Soca. In the west and the Tolminka in the east, the small town Tolmin at the foot of the Vodel mountain in the interior of Slovenia is worth seeing. In addition to the municipal area of the approximately 3,850 inhabitants of the larger city of Tolmin in the upper Soca valley, there are 72 hamlets and villages, all of which are located in the Soca and in the surrounding mountains.

Jukung Bali Bungalow - Indonesien

Jukung Bali Bungalow is located on the breathtakingly beautiful Amed Beach. Look forward to rooms close to the beach, nestled in lush tropical gardens. In-room massage is available upon request, and water sports activities are available. WiFi is available free of charge in all areas. The rooms are equipped with mosquito nets and a refrigerator, as well as a fan or air conditioning. Each of the private terraces is furnished with bamboo furniture and offers sea or garden views. All accommodations are within walking distance of the beach. The private bathroom is fitted with an outdoor shower.

Punta Cana - Dominikanische Republik

Punta Cana liegt an der östlichsten Spitze der Dominikanischen Republik und grenzt an das Karibische Meer und den Atlantik. Charakteristisch für die Region sind die insgesamt 32 km langen Strände und das klare Wasser. Die Gebiete Bávaro und Punta Cana bilden gemeinsam die sogenannte Costa del Coco (Kokosküste), die bekannt ist für ihre exklusiven All-inclusive-Hotelanlagen. Beliebte Freizeitaktivitäten sind Ziplining, Windsurfen, Kajakfahren und Segeln.
